# Smart Card

A **smart card** (**SC**), **chip card**, or **integrated circuit card** (**ICC** or **IC card**), is a card used to control access to a resource. It is typically a plastic credit card-sized card with an embedded integrated circuit (IC) chip.[](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/articles/Smart_card#cite_note-1)
