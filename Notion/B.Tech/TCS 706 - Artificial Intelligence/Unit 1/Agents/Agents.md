An agent is an entity that perceiveits environment through sensors and takes action.
An Agent runs in the cycle of perceiving, thinking, and acting.
Here are the key components of an agent in AI:
1. **Perception:** Agents perceive their environment using sensors or input devices. These sensors could be cameras, microphones, or other types of sensors, depending on the nature of the environment the agent operates in.
2. **Reasoning:** Agents have mechanisms for processing the information received from sensors and making decisions. This can involve various forms of logic, rules, algorithms, or machine learning techniques to deduce useful insights or predict future events based on the available data.
3. **Decision Making:** Based on their reasoning and internal knowledge, agents make decisions or choose actions to perform. The decision-making process can involve complex algorithms, optimization techniques, or heuristics to select the best course of action.
4. **Action:** Agents execute actions in the environment through actuators or output devices. These actions can be physical movements in the case of robots, generating responses in natural language for chatbots, or any other form of interaction suitable for the agent's domain.
5. **Learning:** Many agents are designed to learn from their interactions with the environment. Machine learning algorithms, including supervised learning, reinforcement learning, and deep learning, enable agents to improve their performance over time through experience.

**Sensor**
Sensor is a device which detects the change in the environment and sends the  
information to other electronic devices. An agent observes its environment through sensors.
**Actuators**
Actuators are the component of machines that converts energy into motion. The  
actuators are only responsible for moving and controlling a system. An actuator can be an  
electric motor, gears, rails, etc.
**Effectors**
Effectors are the devices which affect the environment. Effectors can be legs, wheels,  
arms, fingers, wings, fins, and display screen.

### PEAS
PEAS is a type of model on which an AI agent works upon. When we define an AI agent or rational agent, then we can group its properties under PEAS representation model. It is made up of four words:
**P:** Performance measure: Success
**E:** Environment
**A:** Actuators
**S:** Sensors

### Types
1. **Simple reflex agents**
    
    Simple reflex agents are the most basic type of intelligent agents in artificial intelligence
    
    - Based on if-else rule.
    - These agents make decisions solely based on their current percept, without considering the history of past percepts or actions.
    - Works on fully observable environment.
2. Model based agents
    - Uses knowledge base when making decisions.
    - Works on Partially observable environment.
    - Learns.
    - Examples, in automobiles.
3. Goal based agents
    - Extension of model based agents.
    - Works to achieve a specified goals.
    - Uses planning to achieve the target goal.
