Search algorithms can be broadly categorized into two types:
1. Informed (heuristic) search
2. Uninformed (blind) search
### Uninformed Search
- Also known as brute force and blind search.
- Without information.
- Time consuming.
- Higher complexity.
- Examples, BFS, DFS.
**BFS**
- FIFO
- Uses Queue
- Shallow node
- Optimal
**DFS**
- Uses Stack
- LIFO
- Deepest node
- Non optimal
### Informed Search
- With information.
- Faster.
